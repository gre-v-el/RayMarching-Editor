How to
To move metaballs you can grab them and carry around or switch Stationary/Moving switch. Rough/Shiny slider tells the program about shaders, try it! There are also buttons to add or remove random ball. You can also change their sizes.

Something about
Metaballs are literally balls that bend when they are near each other. Adding shaders or fancy colors you can make it more interesting. Coding Train made fantastic video about them. Watch it to learn more!
